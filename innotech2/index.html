Why Choose BACK HOME?

One-Stop Platform

File a proper missing report, generate a professional poster, and notify police/NGOs all at once.

Instant Poster Generation

Create ready-to-share posters with photos, details, QR code, and hotline info in seconds.

Official Connections

Automatically alert nearby police stations and NGOs to make reports official and harder to ignore.

Face Recognition Search

Upload or scan a photo to check if it matches a reported missing person.

Verification = Trust

ID/OTP verification reduces false reports and builds trust in the system.

Centralized Database

Searchable record for volunteers, media, and NGOs to track active cases.

Our Mission

To bring missing loved ones back home by combining technology, awareness, and community action.

Thousands of missing person reports are filed every year. Social media helps only when posts go viral. Our platform ensures every case gets the attention it deserves.
